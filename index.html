Cinna Liselott

Puppeteer

Cinna has been a full time puppeteer in New York for over 14 years, delighting crowds of all ages with her marionettes throughout the city. She truly enjoys constructing unique puppets and coreographing shows for them.

Click the links to find out more about Cinna, her professional background and her curated children's puppetry workshops, perfect for your child's next birthday!

About

Cinna moved to New York 18 years ago with dreams of becoming an actress, but changed courses when she discovered puppetry. She credits them with changing her life in a amazingly positive way.

In her nearly two decades of being a puppeteer, Cinna has had the pleasure of performing at puppet festivals as well as been one of the founding members of a burlesque puppet troupe. She has also been one of the few people to be employed by the City of New York as a puppeteer, teaching schoolchildren how to make puppets and shows of their own.

Workshops

Cinna loves bringing parents and children together in play through puppetry. Each of Cinna's Birthday Party Puppet Workshops are unique and delightful- customized by each child!

In each of Cinna's hour long birthday party workshops, each child gets to make their own pirate, princess or superhero themed puppet and then choreograph a group show for their parents afterwards. Click on the workshops to find out more!
